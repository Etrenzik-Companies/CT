# Funding Diligence

## Purpose
Track lender-readiness and closeout-critical diligence requirements.

## Scope
PoF, capital stack, lender package, third-party reports, final docs, and closing checklist.

## Current Status
🟡 Spec drafted.

## Key Components
- Proof of funds
- Capital stack
- Lender package
- Final funding docs
- Appraisal, market study, environmental, title, survey
- Insurance, legal review, tax review
- Closing checklist and lender dry-run

## Guardrail
Do not treat draft docs as final funding evidence.

## Risks
False readiness due to drafts or missing signoff evidence.

## Next Steps
Implement evidence-backed readiness scoring and blocker registry.
